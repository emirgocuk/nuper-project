Unlock a new dimension for your written content with the Baoyu-Cover-Image agent skill. Designed for creators, this powerful tool transforms ordinary articles into visually engaging experiences by generating unique, hand-drawn style cover images. Whether you're publishing a blog post, a technical deep dive, or a creative story, this skill intuitively analyzes your text to suggest or apply suitable aesthetic styles, ensuring each cover perfectly complements your narrative and captures reader attention instantly. Enhance readability and shareability with bespoke visuals tailored to your content.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Blogging: Quickly generate a captivating cover for a new blog post before publishing.
- •Content Marketing: Create consistent, branded visuals for a series of articles, reports, or newsletters.
- •Technical Documentation: Produce clear, illustrative covers for guides, tutorials, or open-source project READMEs.
- •Social Media Sharing: Design eye-catching images to accompany article links shared across various social platforms.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Experiment with `--style` options: Don't stick to the default; try `blueprint`, `warm`, `dark-atmospheric`, or `minimal` to see which best matches your article's mood and topic.
- 💡Leverage `--aspect` ratios: Use `2.35:1` for a cinematic feel on a landing page, `1:1` for optimal social media presentation, or `16:9` for general widescreen use to maximize visual impact.
- 💡For maximum control, provide clear, concise descriptions of your desired image if using direct text input, or ensure your markdown article has a well-defined title and introduction for best auto-style selection.
