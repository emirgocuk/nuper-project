The baoyu-infographic Agent Skill revolutionizes the creation of professional visual summaries. It stands out by offering a unique two-dimensional approach, allowing users to precisely control both the information structure (layout) and visual aesthetics (style). With 20 distinct layout types and 17 visual styles, it expertly analyzes your content to recommend optimal combinations, producing publication-ready infographics. This skill empowers users to transform complex data or lengthy text into clear, engaging, and impactful visual representations effortlessly.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Summarizing lengthy research papers or business reports into easily digestible visual content for stakeholders.
- •Creating compelling marketing materials to explain product features, service benefits, or statistical insights.
- •Presenting complex project timelines, workflows, or organizational structures in a clear, graphical format.
- •Developing educational content that simplifies difficult concepts for students or training participants.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Start with the skill's auto-recommendation feature (`/baoyu-infographic path/to/content.md`) to quickly get a suitable layout and style combination based on your content's nature.
- 💡Experiment with specific `--layout` options like 'funnel' for processes or 'hierarchical-layers' for structures to ensure the infographic accurately reflects the underlying data relationships.
- 💡Leverage the `--aspect` option (portrait, landscape, square) to optimize your infographic for different output platforms, such as social media, presentations, or print publications.
