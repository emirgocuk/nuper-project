Unlock the power of visually stunning presentations directly within your coding environment. This AI agent skill simplifies the complex process of crafting Marp-based slides, transforming your raw content into polished, professional decks. Leverage intelligent theme selection and automatic design enhancements to produce impactful presentations without manual design efforts. Whether for technical talks, business pitches, or academic lectures, this skill ensures your message is delivered clearly and attractively, saving you valuable time and effort in slide preparation.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Quickly create professional presentation slides or Marp documents from your content.
- •Improve the design and visual appeal of existing slide drafts with minimal effort.
- •Generate lecture or seminar materials tailored to specific themes like 'tech' or 'business'.
- •Produce bullet-point focused slides that incorporate images effectively and aesthetically.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always specify your desired theme (e.g., 'tech', 'business', 'colorful') or the overall tone of your content to guide the AI for optimal visual results.
- 💡For slides requiring complex image layouts or specific visual patterns, explicitly instruct the AI to consult its `references/image-patterns.md` documentation.
- 💡Leverage phrases like 'make it look good' or 'improve the design' for iterative refinements; the skill is designed to enhance visual quality automatically.
