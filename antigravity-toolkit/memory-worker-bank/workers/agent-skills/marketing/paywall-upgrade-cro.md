Unlock the full revenue potential of your product with the Paywall & Upgrade CRO Agent Skill. Designed to help you strategically design and optimize in-app paywalls, upgrade screens, and upsell modals, this skill guides you in converting free users to paid subscribers or upgrading existing customers to higher tiers. By focusing on critical in-product moments, it ensures that your conversion flows are intuitive, value-driven, and seamlessly integrated into the user journey, maximizing your freemium and trial conversion rates without alienating your audience. Leverage expert principles to boost your product's monetization.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Optimizing an existing freemium conversion funnel to increase paid subscriptions.
- •Designing a new in-app paywall for a recently introduced premium feature.
- •Improving the conversion rate of a trial expiration screen or usage limit prompt.
- •Crafting effective upsell modals to encourage users to move to higher-tier plans.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always demonstrate significant value before presenting an upgrade prompt; users convert when they understand what they're gaining.
- 💡A/B test different messaging, visual cues, and pricing presentations within your in-app modals to continuously optimize conversion rates.
- 💡Map the user journey to pinpoint 'aha moments' and trigger upgrade offers precisely when users are experiencing the most value.
