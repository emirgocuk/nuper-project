Unlock the power of your customer base with the Referral & Affiliate Programs skill. This agent is designed for marketers, founders, and growth professionals seeking to build scalable acquisition channels. Whether you're launching a new incentive-based strategy or optimizing an existing one, this skill guides you through best practices for program design, incentive structures, and performance analysis. Transform loyal customers into brand advocates and drive exponential growth through proven viral loops.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Designing a new customer referral program from scratch, including incentive models and terms.
- •Optimizing an underperforming affiliate program by analyzing current metrics and suggesting improvements.
- •Developing a comprehensive word-of-mouth strategy to increase organic customer acquisition and brand advocacy.
- •Benchmarking existing referral or affiliate incentives against industry standards to identify competitive advantages and areas for improvement.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always clarify your target audience and program goals (e.g., new customer acquisition, retention, brand awareness) before initiating program design to ensure alignment.
- 💡Experiment with A/B testing different incentive structures (e.g., cash, discounts, exclusive access) for both referrer and referred to find what resonates best with your audience.
- 💡Integrate the referral program seamlessly into the customer journey, making it easy and intuitive for users to participate, share, and track their referrals.
