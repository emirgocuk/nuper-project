# launch-strategy
This skill empowers AI coding assistants to act as a seasoned marketing strategist, guiding users through the intricacies of a successful product or feature launch. From crafting compelling announcements to strategizing for early access and building sustained momentum, it covers the full spectrum of go-to-market activities. Leverage this expertise to ensure your innovations not only reach your audience but also captivate them, turning initial interest into active engagement and growth. It's designed to transform every release into a strategic opportunity for impact.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always start with your 'Owned Channels' first to build a direct relationship and compound value over time.

- 💡Leverage the 'ORB Framework' (Owned, Rented, Borrowed) to ensure a balanced and effective channel strategy.
