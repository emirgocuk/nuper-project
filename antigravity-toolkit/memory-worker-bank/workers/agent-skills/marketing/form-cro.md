The `form-cro` agent skill empowers AI assistants to become expert strategists in enhancing conversion rates for various online forms. Beyond just basic field validation, this skill provides comprehensive guidance on form design, user experience, and psychological triggers to minimize abandonment. It equips the AI with an understanding of different form types and their unique optimization challenges, ensuring tailored recommendations that balance data collection needs with user effort. Leverage this skill to transform your forms into powerful conversion engines.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Optimizing a multi-step application form to reduce drop-offs and improve applicant completion rates.
- •Improving conversion rates for a gated content lead capture form by identifying and removing friction points.
- •Redesigning a checkout form to minimize abandoned carts and streamline the purchase process.
- •Getting recommendations to simplify a complex B2B demo request form while ensuring essential data is collected.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always specify the *type* of form (e.g., lead gen, contact, checkout) and its *current completion rate* for the most targeted advice from the AI.
- 💡Be prepared to discuss the *business goal* behind each form field – the AI can help cut unnecessary fields if it understands their ultimate purpose.
- 💡Ask for A/B testing suggestions on specific form elements like field labels, CTA button text, or progress indicators to continuously improve performance.
