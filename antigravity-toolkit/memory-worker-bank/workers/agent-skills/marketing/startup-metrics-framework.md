Unlock the power of data-driven decision-making for your startup with this comprehensive agent skill. Designed for founders, product managers, and growth teams, it demystifies essential business performance indicators across various models, from SaaS to marketplaces. Gain clarity on your unit economics, growth efficiency, and cash management, ensuring you're tracking the right metrics at every stage—from seed to Series A and beyond. Leverage its insights to optimize your strategy, impress investors, and achieve sustainable growth.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Analyzing a SaaS startup's monthly recurring revenue (MRR) and churn rate.
- •Calculating and interpreting Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and Lifetime Value (LTV) for a new product launch.
- •Benchmarking a startup's growth rate against industry standards for fundraising.
- •Understanding and applying advanced metrics like 'Rule of 40' or 'Burn Multiple' for financial health assessment.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always specify your business model (e.g., SaaS, marketplace, consumer) when asking, as metrics definitions and benchmarks can vary significantly.
- 💡Combine metric calculations with scenario analysis (e.g., 'What if churn decreases by 10%?') for deeper insights into potential impact.
- 💡Request real-world examples or industry benchmarks to contextualize your own metrics against successful companies.
