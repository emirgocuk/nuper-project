# When to Use This Skill
- •Developing a pricing model for a new SaaS product launch.
- •Optimizing existing pricing tiers to improve conversion rates or ARPU.
- •Analyzing competitor pricing and identifying differentiation opportunities.
- •Designing or refining freemium, free trial, or value-based monetization strategies.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always provide detailed business context from the 'Before Starting' section to get the most tailored recommendations.
- 💡Focus on identifying a strong 'value metric' that aligns with how customers derive value, rather than just seat-based pricing.
