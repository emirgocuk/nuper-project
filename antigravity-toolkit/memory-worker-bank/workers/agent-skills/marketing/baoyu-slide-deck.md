Unleash the power of AI to transform your raw content into polished, visually appealing slide deck images instantly. The Baoyu Slide Deck Generator Agent Skill streamlines the often time-consuming process of creating presentations. Whether you're drafting a proposal for executives, explaining concepts to beginners, or just need a quick visual aid, this skill offers robust customization options. Define your audience, choose from various aesthetic styles, and even specify the number of slides, all while maintaining a professional look without manual design effort. Elevate your communication with stunning presentations, effortlessly generated.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Rapidly create a professional presentation from meeting notes, a research paper, or a markdown document.
- •Generate a styled pitch deck for investors or clients, tailored to a specific audience like 'executives'.
- •Produce engaging training materials or educational slides by customizing visual styles and audience types.
- •Visualize complex data or concepts quickly for internal team communication or external reporting.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always structure your input content clearly (e.g., using Markdown headings) to help the skill generate a logical outline.
- 💡Experiment with different `--style` and `--audience` combinations to find the perfect visual tone for your message.
- 💡Utilize the `--outline-only` option first to refine your presentation structure before committing to image generation.
- 💡After generating slides, use `scripts/merge-to-pptx.ts` or `scripts/merge-to-pdf.ts` for convenient distribution and final polish.
