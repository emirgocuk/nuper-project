Launching a new digital venture often starts with the perfect domain name, a critical asset for online identity and branding. This AI agent skill streamlines the traditionally tedious process of finding a memorable and available web address. Instead of manual searches and endless 'name taken' messages, leverage intelligent generation and real-time availability checks across popular TLDs. It's designed to spark creativity while ensuring practicality, saving entrepreneurs and developers valuable time in their journey to establish an online presence.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Rapidly naming a new startup, product, or service.
- •Finding unique domain alternatives when primary choices are taken.
- •Securing a professional and memorable domain for a personal brand or portfolio.
- •Brainstorming catchy names for a side project, blog, or temporary campaign.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Provide a detailed description of your project's core purpose and target audience for more highly relevant and tailored suggestions.
- 💡Experiment with different keywords, desired tones (e.g., 'playful', 'professional', 'modern'), and naming conventions (e.g., 'short & catchy', 'descriptive') in your prompts.
- 💡Ask the skill to focus on specific TLDs like '.com' or '.io' if you have a strong preference, or explore less common options like '.xyz' for unique availability.
