This specialized agent skill empowers AI coding assistants to streamline the creation of essential internal communications. Designed to help teams and organizations maintain clear, consistent messaging, it provides structured guidelines for various document types. By leveraging this skill, users can quickly draft professional updates, reports, and FAQs that align with established company formats, significantly boosting productivity and ensuring organizational coherence. It eliminates the guesswork in formatting and tone, allowing teams to focus on content accuracy and timely dissemination.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Drafting comprehensive 3P updates for project teams and stakeholders.
- •Generating engaging company-wide newsletters for important announcements.
- •Crafting clear and concise FAQ responses for common employee inquiries.
- •Producing structured incident reports for post-mortems and learning.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always specify the exact type of internal communication needed (e.g., 'incident report' vs. 'general update') to ensure the correct template is applied.
- 💡Provide key facts, dates, and relevant stakeholders upfront to ensure the output is highly relevant and accurate.
- 💡Review the generated draft and refine it for specific company voice or sensitive details before final distribution.
