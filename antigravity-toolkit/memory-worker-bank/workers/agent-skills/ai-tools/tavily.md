The Tavily Agent Skill equips AI coding assistants with a robust interface for real-time web interaction. It's designed to go beyond basic search, offering advanced capabilities like structured content extraction, site mapping, and in-depth research. This skill ensures that your AI can access the latest online information, process it effectively for complex tasks, and present results in an LLM-friendly format, enhancing the intelligence and utility of your AI-powered development workflows. Integrate Tavily to bring dynamic, up-to-date information directly into your agent's toolkit.

# When to Use This Skill
- •Gathering live web information for a coding project or research task.
- •Extracting specific content from websites or mapping site structures programmatically.
- •Performing multi-step investigations that require fresh, structured web data.
- •Summarizing web content with sources and citations for reporting or documentation.

# Pro Tips
- 💡Always specify `search_depth` in your Tavily requests (e.g., 'basic', 'advanced') to control the thoroughness and cost of your search.
- 💡Leverage Tavily's `include_answer` and `include_raw_content` parameters to get both a concise summary and the full context for deeper analysis by your LLM.
- 💡For complex research, chain Tavily searches with internal knowledge retrieval or other tools to synthesize comprehensive answers.
